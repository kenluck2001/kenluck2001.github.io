Here is a select list of open source projects

pySmooth: A unique time series library in Python that consists of Kalman filters (discrete, extended, and unscented), online ARIMA (novel idea as a personal invention), and time difference model, 2017.

Plant Monitoring: I have developed models for predicting the amount of CO2 emission from power plant in a research project that is sponsored by Nigerian Liquefied Natural Gas Treatment plant (Train 4). The models consist of MARS (Multivariate adaptive regression splines), decision tree, neural networks, and ANFIS (Adaptive neuro fuzzy inference system), 2017.

Anomaly: I have written a small library for real-time anomaly detection using a redis store to ensure persistence of summary parameters between sliding windows, 2017.
